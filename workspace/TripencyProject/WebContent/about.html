Overview

Tripency is a website designed to create the perfect trip itinerary. This site is designed to help people who have a desire to travel and trust for discovery. App allows users to locate places of interest and attractions in their selected destinations that may otherwise remain undiscovered. This site is also set to help people who know the destination they plan to travel too but lack knowledge of what the attractions the Country/Cities have to offer. In this website it will ask you for your general area of interested to help generate an itinerary that is personal to each user.

Audience

Our audience will be users who are either not interested in planning every details of a trip. Or for the users who do not have the time to plan a trip itinerary. Some times this could be caused by the lack of knowledge on the City or Country they plan to visit. People who are visiting places for the first time would benefit from this site as it has a large variety of attractions for a large variety of cities. People on short trips that do not want to waste time and energy on planning .
